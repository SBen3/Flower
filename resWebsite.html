Our Story

FLWER began as a small dream shared between 'Alex sider'. Growing up surrounded by vibrant gardens, we've always held a deep appreciation for the beauty and joy that flowers bring. In 1990, we decided to turn our passion into a reality, opening our first shop in 'Some place'.

Over the years, FLWER has blossomed into a cherished part of the community. We believe in using the freshest, highest quality blooms to create stunning arrangements for every occasion. More than just flowers, we strive to deliver happiness and create lasting memories for our customers.

It all started with a renegade sunflower and a dream to spread a little sunshine. Yep, you heard that right! Back in 1990, 'alex sider' stumbled upon this giant, glorious sunflower in their backyard. Inspired by its larger-than-life personality, they decided to share the flower magic with the world.

Today, FLWER is a vibrant online and in-store flower haven. We're all about pushing the boundaries of floral design, creating Insta-worthy arrangements that are as unique as you are. So, whether you're looking for a statement bouquet or a whimsical centerpiece, we've got you covered.
